Australian BioCommons Technical Documentation

This site presents a landscape view of tools & workflows available across Australian BioCommons partners.

What resources are available here?

Note: you can reach this information using the tabs at the top of the page as well.

  • Visit ToolFinder to discover which versions of bioinformatics tools are installed across several of the BioCommons infrastructure partner systems.
  • Visit WorkflowFinder to search for workflows registered by Australian BioCommons partners on workflowhub.
  • A list of the Australian BioCommons infrastructure partner compute systems is available here.
  • For Help please visit the Australian BioCommons Support page.

We welcome feedback: please provide your suggestions / comments via GoogleForm or GitHub issues

Acknowledgements

The information presented here is part of collaborative activities coordinated by the Australian BioCommons. These activities draw upon the expertise and enthusiasm of the BioCommons partner infrastructures and the Australian bioinformatics community, which includes:

  • The BioCommons communities (e.g.genome assembly, metabolomics)
  • The Bioplatforms Australia Framework Initiatives (e.g.Oz Mammals Genomics, Genomics for Australian Plants)

The website and its contents are built using python, pycharm, R and the tidyverse (dplyr et al.), with references included, and additional attributions embedded in the Rmarkdown scripts.
